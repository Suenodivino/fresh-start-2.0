70 Million Jobs

70 Million Jobs is the first national for-profit employment platform to help those with records find employment. Their goal is to present great companies that offer second chance jobs for people with criminal records.

Jails to Jobs

Jails to Jobs, a Section 501(c)(3) nonprofit, is an organization that gives previously incarcerated and soon-to-be-released men and women the tools they need to find employment. It educates and informs this population through workshops in jails and prisons in Northern California. The organization also maintains a searchable directory of free and low-cost tattoo removal programs across the U.S. that now has more than 300 programs in 42 states.
